How to play

How do I play?

In the homepage click Play or click here! Then choose the artist you want to test your skills on, pick a difficulty and our algorithm will pick the song and lyric for you! Enjoy =]

How many artists are in your game?

Since Falsetto uses LastFM and LRCLIB, the count is likely to be over 100 million! From your local niche bands to the most known and beloved bands in the world.

I found a bug in your game, what to do?

For any unwanted errors/bugs, please report them to the team! For more info about contacting us, go to our contact page
