Main & Gameplay

How do I play?

From the homepage, click Play or click right here! Choose the artist you want to test your skills on, pick a difficulty and Falsetto's algorithm will generate a lyric snippet for you to guess.

Do I need to register or log in to play?

No! And that is the beauty of Falsetto. As a security enthusiast, I prioritize user privacy. You can jump straight into the game and play completely anonymously!

How many bands and singers are available?

Since Falsetto grabs the music from LastFM and the lyrics from LRCLIB, our database covers millions and millions of songs. From your local niche band to global pop icons!

I found a bug in the game, what should I do?

For any unwanted errors, bugs or glitches, please report them to me! Include the steps you did before and the details about the bug. You can find more info on contacting me on the contact page.

Account & Privacy

Does this site collect any of my data?

No! And it never will and that is the beauty of Falsetto. As a security enthusiast, I prioritize user privacy.
If you choose to register, your credentials are securely hashed and the only data that is kept, is your score and profile info that you put in!

Can I delete my account and all associated data with it?

Absolutely! If you did make an account and want to wipe the slate clean, you can completely delete everything from your Account Settings, or by contacting me via the contact page. Your account and everything related to it will be wiped permanently.

Contributions & Collaboration

How can I help you or collaborate with you on this project?

I really appreciate the support! If you are a developer, designer or just have any ideas for improvement and want to collaborate, drop me a message, more info in the contact page! I will be glad to talk! :]

Can I fix incorrect lyrics or add missing songs?

Yes, but that is out of my hands! The songs are grabbed from LastFM and the lyrics from LRCLIB. For more info look on their sites!
