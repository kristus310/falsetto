About Falsetto

A privacy-first music trivia game built for lyric lovers, casual listeners, and tech enthusiasts.

What is Falsetto?

Falsetto is an open-source web game designed to push your music knowledge to its limits. By generating dynamic, real-time lyric snippets from your favorite artists, Falsetto tests how well you truly know the songs spinning in your headphones.

Whether you want to test your knowledge on a local niche indie band or global pop icons, our seamless platform generation serves up customized challenges across varying difficulty levels.

Our Core Philosophy

Privacy-First Design

No trackers, no surveillance, no bloated data storage. Play seamlessly as a complete anonymous user without leaving a digital footprint.

Infinite Library

By pulling metadata and lyric configurations programmatically via trusted open developer platforms, our game catalog spans millions of songs.

The Tech Stack

Falsetto is engineered with a modern, lightweight architectural philosophy. It scales entirely on server-rendered efficiency with a snappy front-end user experience:
