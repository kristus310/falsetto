Privacy Policy

As a security-minded developer, user privacy is paramount. Falsetto is designed around data minimization.

What data do we collect?

  • Anonymous Players: We do not collect, track, or save any personal identifier data. You play completely anonymously.
  • Registered Users: If you sign up, we save only your chosen username, your high scores/points, and your cryptographically hashed credentials (passwords are safely salted and hashed).

Data Deletion & Rights

You own your data. You can wipe your account data instantly at any time directly through your Account Settings panel or by reaching out via our Contact Page. Once initiated, all your profile data and score records are permanently deleted from our database.
